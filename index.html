Simple Web App with A/B Testing and Sticky User Assignment

This project reuses the earlier Google OIDC login demo and extends it into a Week 6 submission. After sign-in, users are randomly assigned to Home Page A or Home Page B, kept on that version, and their button clicks are recorded with a timestamp, IP address, and page version.
